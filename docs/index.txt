            <h3>Introduction</h3>
            <p class="sub-title">
                CultForge is a community-driven Minecraft launcher that offers more than just starting
                the game — it fosters a fully synchronized, immersive multiplayer experience. By integrating
                mods, shaders, and resource packs that are pre-configured and seamlessly synced with server
                settings and live status, CultForge enables players to enjoy a consistent and engaging gameplay
                environment. Whether you're competing on leaderboards, collaborating on missions, or simply
                exploring, CultForge brings the community together with cutting-edge features designed for
                today's Minecraft enthusiasts.
            </p>
